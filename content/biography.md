I am a Research Professor in the Department of Information Security at Sejong University and a member of SysCore Lab. I received my Ph.D. in Information Security from Sejong University in 2023.

After completing my doctorate, I continued at Sejong University as a Post-Doctoral researcher from 2023 to 2024 and have been serving as a Research Professor since 2024. My work focuses on building and analyzing secure systems with emphasis on system security, cloud security, malware analysis, and AI-related threats and defenses.

My current research connects practical system analysis with emerging security challenges. I am particularly interested in hypervisors, system monitoring, system memory analysis, malware analysis, deep learning, and resilient security methodologies for modern computing environments.
